Three-Day Nature & Adventure Tour

Day 1: Immerse yourself in traditional village life with cultural activities, cooking, weaving, a dip in a natural pool, and a treehouse stay under the stars.
Day 2: Trek to Yahangala (Bedrock of Kings) with breathtaking views, spot wild elephants, and stay in a treehouse amidst chena cultivation.
Day 3: Enjoy exhilarating waterfall sliding, explore a charming local village, and return to Kandy.
Highlights: Treehouse stays, trekking, wildlife spotting, cultural immersion, and nature adventures.

Explore the Pekoe Trail

Overview: Join Ranger.LK on the Pekoe Trail, a 300 km trek through Sri Lanka's central highlands. Experience diverse landscapes, cultural connections, and sustainable travel.
Trail Highlights:

  • Day 1: Trek from Hanthana to Galaha, with panoramic views.
  • Day 2-3: Explore tea estates, Horton Plains, and Lipton's Seat.
  • Cultural Stops: Engage with local communities and tea culture, visit tea factories and villages.
  • Signature Treks: Hanthana to Galaha, Nuwara Eliya to Ella, and cultural stops at tea estates.
Why Choose This Trail? Stunning landscapes, tea culture, sustainability, guided tours, and flexible itineraries.
Best Months to Visit: January to April and July to August.

Five-Day Adventure Expedition

Overview: A 5-day trek from Kandy to Sigiriya via Meemure, Ranamure, Walpolamulla, and Pitawala.
Day 1: Depart from Kandy, trek to hidden waterfalls, and explore Meemure village culture. Overnight stay at a Meemure homestay.
Day 2: Trek to Ranamure through forests and streams. Overnight stay in a cabana.
Day 3: Observe wildlife on a trek to Walpolamulla. Explore village ruins. Overnight stay at a Walpolamulla homestay.
Day 4: Climb Manigala Mountain and descend to Pitawala Village. Overnight stay at a Pitawala homestay.
Day 5: Visit Pitawala Pathana, Mini World's End, and Bambarakiri Ella Waterfall. Continue to Sigiriya Rock Fortress.
Highlights: Trekking, hidden waterfalls, village exploration, wildlife spotting, and cultural immersion.

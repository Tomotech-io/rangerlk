Three-Day Nature and Adventure Tour by Ranger.LK

Immerse Yourself in Nature, Culture, and Adventure

Day 1: Immersion in Traditional Village Life

Scenic drives, cultural activities (cooking, weaving), a dip in a serene natural pool, and an overnight stay in a rustic treehouse with dinner under the stars.

Day 2: Trekking to Yahangala and Wildlife Adventure

Adventurous trek to Yahangala (Bedrock of Kings), wild elephant observation, and an overnight stay in a chena treehouse, surrounded by traditional farming practices.

Day 3: Waterfall Sliding and Village Exploration

Exhilarating waterfall sliding adventure and exploration of a charming local village. Includes lunch before departure for Kandy.

Tour Highlights: Traditional village life, treehouse stays, trekking Yahangala, wild elephant sightings, waterfall sliding, and cultural immersion.
